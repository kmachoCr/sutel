Subscription TV
The subscription TV service experienced changes during the 2011-2013 period. Due to the impact of this situation on the telecommunication markets, it must be analyzed. The amount of subscriptions to this service increased by 42 %; this results not only in the increased growth of this market, but also in the expansion of other telecommunication services, such as Internet and VoIP, both individually and in packages. In this regard, 34 % of the subscription TV service providers also offer access to Internet services and 10 % of them offer Internet access and VoIP as well.

Furthermore, although most TV services are Cable TV, Satellite TV shows an upward trend. Also, Internet Protocol Television (IPTV) and Multipoint Microwave Distribution Services (MMDS) appear as new choices in the market.
